Our Story

It started as a dream to save together... In a small African community, a group of market traders came together to form a "contribution circle". Each week, they contributed a part of their earnings to a trusted leader, Mama Nkechi, who would manage the savings.

For months, it worked perfectly. Everyone relied on the system. It helped members pay school fees, start businesses, and even save for emergencies.

But then the unimaginable happened. One morning, Mama Nkechi didn't show up. She had passed away unexpectedly, leaving the group in chaos. The savings, the trust, and the dreams vanished overnight.

This isn't just one story - it's a reality for millions. What if there were a way to save together without fear of loss, bias, or human error?

What if Mama Nkechi was using SaveHub?

What if savings could be automated, secure, and inclusive? No single point of failure. No discrimination. No human risks.

That's where SaveHub comes in: We are building the future of savings for everyone, everywhere.

The Traditional Savings Challenge

The Problem
- Lack of Trust: In traditional group savings, funds are entrusted to an individual who may misuse or mismanage them.
- Discrimination: Limited inclusivity based on race, gender, or socio-economic status.
- Unforeseen Risks: Events like death or fraud jeopardize group savings.
- Limited Reach: Savings groups are often geographically restricted.

Our Solution

Trust Through Technology: Secure and automated contributions eliminate human risks.
Inclusive Groups: Verified members from any location, promoting diversity.
Flexible Plans: Savings groups with customizable contributions and timelines.
Digital Transformation: Accessible anytime, anywhere with seamless payments.

"Imagine Mama Nkechi's circle with SaveHub." Instead of one person managing funds, SaveHub securely automates everything. Contributions are tracked, payments are guaranteed, and group members can connect, grow, and save without fear of loss or bias.

How It Works

1. Users create a verified profile.
2. Choose a savings plan (daily, weekly, monthly).
3. Get grouped with 4 other verified users in a Hive.
4. Contributions are automated and visible to all group members.
5. Earn points and badges for consistency, referrals, and engagement.
6. Redeem points for rewards and climb leaderboards!
